About Me

I am a undergraduate student majoring in Physics and Astronomy & Astrophysics at UCSD.

My research interests fall in plasma turbulence under ExB shearing and the basics of energy transport as well as Alfvén waves. I am currently studying the concept of Kubo number and the production ratio in turbulent region near the Last Closed Flux Surface (LCFS) of tokamaks.

I previously worked at Summer Program for Undergraduate Research in Science (SPURS) in the group of Prof. Patrick Diamond (2024-2027).

Outside of the field of plasma physics, I also enjoy playing basketball, swimming, and playing snooker with my peers.
